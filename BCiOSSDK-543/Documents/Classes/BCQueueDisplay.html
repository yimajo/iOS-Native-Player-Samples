actionAtItemEnd

Action to take when the end of one queued video is reached. Behaves identically to AVQueuePlayer’s actionAtItemEnd property. The component will take this value from the ‘action’ property of a BCEventSetActionAtItemEnd’s details.

@property (nonatomic, readonly) AVPlayerActionAtItemEnd actionAtItemEnd

Discussion

Valid values are: – AVPlayerActionAtItemEndAdvance = 0, (default) – AVPlayerActionAtItemEndPause = 1, – AVPlayerActionAtItemEndNone = 2,
