Other Methods

  • – stopPropagation

    When called, will cause the event to stop begin emitted to any other objects that are listening for this event.
  • – preventDefault

    When called, will cause the event’s defaultPrevented property to become true permenantly. Any listener may call preventDefault to signal to the original caller of emit that whatever action it would have normally taken should not happen.
