Properties

position

The position at which this cue point will fire.

@property (nonatomic, readonly) NSString *position

Discussion

Possible values:

  • “before” – fired immediately before playback begins at the start of a video. “begin” cue points will fire again if the playhead is reset to the starting position.
  • “after” – fired immediately after a video ends. “after” cue points will fire again if the end of the video is reached more than once.
  • The number of seconds after which the cue point should fire, e.g. @“4.125”
